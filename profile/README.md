> **The boss of them all\* since 2026**

### The Origin
I played my first computer games on an **Apple II GS** and never looked back. In the high shareware days of the nineties, you could play amazing games for free, developed and published by people who wanted to play them. 

I first heard of the **“boss key”** looking through the manual for one of these, now lost to time and memory: *An unused key that replaced whatever was on the screen with something that looked like work.* I may have forgotten the game, but the **Boss Button** stuck with me.

### Our Manifesto
Boss Button Studios is about recapturing the independent spirit of software development. We build software we want to use and are proud to share. Maybe we can make a little money back for the effort. 

**Software that actually works for you.**

* **Transparency First:** If our software must take anything from you for our benefit, we will tell you up front. We stand behind that with **publicly auditable code**.
* **Vibe Coding:** We are unapologetic vibe coders. We believe in AI as a democratizing tool to make more people into creators.
* **Ethical Creation:** We do not publish anything that steals from artists. Any AI-generated art will iterate from the start on work we own.
* **Value Creation:** We are not a non-profit, but profit isn't the point. The mission is value creation, not value capture.
* **The Village Hustle:** We believe that love, family, and community are the foundations of true success. Our timelines reflect that.
* **Irreverence:** We take our work seriously, but not ourselves.
