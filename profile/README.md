Boss Button Studios

"The boss of them all* since 2026."

I played my first computer games on an Apple II GS and never looked back. In the high shareware days of the nineties you could play amazing games for free, developed and published by people who wanted to play them. I first heard of the “boss key” looking through the manual for one of these, now lost to time and memory. An unused key that replaced whatever was on the screen with something that looked like work. I may have forgotten the game but the boss button stuck with me.

Boss Button Studios is about recapturing the independent spirit of software development. Software we want to use and are happy to share and maybe make a little money back for the effort. Software that actually works for you. 

If our software must take anything from you for our benefit, we will tell you up front about it. We will stand behind that with publicly auditable code.

We are unapologetic vibe coders. We believe in “AI” as a democratizing tool to make more people into creators. 

We do not publish anything that steals from artists.

We are not a non-profit, but profit isn't the point. The mission is value creation, not value capture.

We take out work seriously, but not ourselves.

This is a company dedicated to the village hustle - we believe that love, family, and community are the foundations of true success, and our timelines will reflect that.
